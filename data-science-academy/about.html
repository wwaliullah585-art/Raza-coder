Meet Our CEO

Raza Wazir

Raza Wazir is the visionary founder and CEO of Data Science Academy. With a passion for technology and education, he established this academy to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Under his leadership, Data Science Academy has grown from a small local initiative in Bannu, KPK to a recognized online platform serving students across Pakistan and beyond.

His mission is to make quality technology education accessible to everyone, regardless of their geographic location or financial background.

Our Mission

To provide world-class technology education that empowers students to build successful careers in data science, programming, and AI.

Our Vision

To become the leading online academy in Pakistan for technology education, producing industry-ready professionals who drive innovation.

Our Values

Excellence, accessibility, innovation, and community. We believe every student deserves the opportunity to learn and grow.

Our Location

Based in Bannu, KPK, Pakistan. We serve students locally and globally through our online platform.

Achievements

500+ students trained, 50+ courses offered, partnerships with industry leaders, and a growing community of tech professionals.
